What is this about?

A cowboy rides out in the morning on his horse, and returns in the evening.

return.horse is an experimental webcomic. It's built upon many of the standard webcomic conventions that I love. Some of the things I'm trying to explore are:

  1. creative repetition
  2. lowering the bar for creating something
  3. the webcomic process
  4. the ontological nature of a webcomic
Ultimately I made this because I thought it would be funny. The rest kind of followed from that.
